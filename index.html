Biography

I am a first-year PhD student in the Computer Science and Engineering Division at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, supervised by Prof. Barzan Mozafari. Previously, I received the M.Phil. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) under the supervision of Prof. James Cheng and Prof. Ming-Chang Yang in 2020 and received the B.Sc. degree from the same Department at CUHK in 2018.

Currently I am exploring zero-shot learning (ZSL) algorithms. Previously I explored the similarity search area, including Nearest Neighbor Search (NNS) and Maximum Inner Product Search (MIPS). Recently, My research paper about the graph-based MIPS method was accepted by AAAI-20.
